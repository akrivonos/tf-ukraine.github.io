The purpose of the association is to support Ukrainian pupils and students whose educational path is endangered by the war or whose education was interrupted. Another purpose is to promote further education and job market integration of unemployed graduates in Ukraine.

  • Organization of lecture events and discussion forums for arranging educational options and qualified professional orientation;
  • Cooperation with other foundations, associations from the fields of education, science and business;
  • Granting of short-term scholarships for pupils and students with outstanding achievements or social commitment;
  • Financing of studies, further training and vocational courses for junior staff in precarious situations;
  • Procurement of learning materials and access to learning platforms.
